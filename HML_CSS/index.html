Captioning is the process of converting the audio content of a video file into text, then displaying the text on that video, most time in the bottom.
There are 2 types of captions: open and closed captions.

Open Caption

qsdfsdfsdf

Open captions are permanently fixed to the video, meaning that you can’t switch them off/on. They are mostly used on social media, or when the video player does not have [CC] functionality.

Closed Caption

qsdfsdfsdf

Closed captions are almost the same with Open captions, except they can be enabled by the user watching the video only when needed. You can mostly see them on Youtube, Vimeo, Netflix etc...
